Site Purpose

This site is made to deliver quick and simple weather forecasts to people of all walks of life. The purpose of this website is to cause weather lookups online to be easy and comfortable.

Target Audience

The target audience for this website is a number of people in a number of careers, ages and cultures. The website targets people who want to know the weather now, and in the coming days in the cities of Greenfield, Springville and Franklin.

Persona

Guy Booker is a logger in Idaho and uses weather forecasts to see what the week will be like, as he often leaves every other week into the mountains for days at a time and cant check it there.

Quote: "I want to know what the weather will be like in the next week, because Im in the mountains all week and need to know what to expect."
